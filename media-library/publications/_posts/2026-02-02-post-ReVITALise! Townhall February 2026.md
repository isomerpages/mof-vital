<h4>ReVITALise! Townhall February 2026: Celebrating Our People &amp; Progress</h4>
<p>VITAL’s bi-annual ReVITALise! Townhall brought VITALites together to celebrate
achievements, share key updates, and connect as one community.
<br>
<br>The session recognised colleagues who received the VITAL Transformation
Awards and the Go the Extra Mile Service (GEMS) Awards, and featured updates
on organisational efforts to strengthen AI literacy and career development
as part of building a future-ready workforce. It also marked the kick-off
of VITAL’s 20th anniversary celebrations and concluded with a meaningful
dialogue with senior management.
<br>
<br>Thank you to all VITALites who made ReVITALise! a success. Here’s to an
exciting 2026 ahead!</p>
