<p>The MOF-Ministry Family Data Symposium at Revenue House last Friday featured
insightful exchanges. Teams from IRAS, Customs, and ACRA presented various
challenges, solutions, and learnings. Additionally, Google delivered a
presentation, followed by a panel discussion on the "Opportunities and
Challenges of Analytics and Generative AI."</p>
<p>GD (F&amp;P) Kavitha Subash represented VITAL as a panelist, sharing valuable
insights on the transformative potential of Analytics and Generative AI.
It was truly a productive session!</p>
