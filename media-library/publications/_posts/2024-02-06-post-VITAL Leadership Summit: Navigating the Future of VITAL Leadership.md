<p>Under the theme "Navigating the Future of VITAL Leadership", leaders in
VITAL engaged in an enriching afternoon of insights and open discussions,
culminating in an interactive team activity involving flying drones and
building drone paths. This hands-on experience added a dynamic dimension
to the summit, fostering collaboration and innovation.</p>
<p>Together, valuable perspectives and strategies were gained to lead with
resilience and innovation. The active participation and shared experiences
have undeniably contributed to making this summit a remarkable success.</p>
<p>A special thank you is extended to Mr. Hock Keong Ng, DCE (CPFB), our
Keynote speaker, and to our HRD colleagues for their dedication in planning
and orchestrating a memorable and impactful VITAL Leadership Summit.</p>
