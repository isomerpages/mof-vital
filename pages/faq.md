### **FAQ**

    <li><p><div><b>Does VITAL offer internship opportunities for tertiary students?</b></div>Yes. For interested applicants, please do email <a href="mailto:vital_recruit@vital.gov.sg" target="_blank">VITAL Recruit</a>.</p>
